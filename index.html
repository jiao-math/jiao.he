I am currently an assistant professor in Laboratoire de Mathématiques d'Orsay at the Université Paris-Saclay.

A bref introduction of my research -- "Five Minute Talks" at MSRI.

I was a Postdoctoral Fellowship in the program "Mathematical problems in fluid dynamics" at MSRI, Berkeley, from January 19 to May 28, 2021. I worked under the supervision of David Lannes. From October 2019 to August 2021, I was a Post-doc researcher funded by the Programme Sophie Germain of FMJH in LaMME at the University of Evry. I worked under the supervision of Diego Chamorro. I finished my Ph.D In September 2019 in Institute Camille Jordan at the University Bernard Claude Lyon 1 under the supervise of Dragoş Iftimie and Lorenzo Brandolese. My dissertation is available Here.

I am currently working on the analysis of partial differential equations :
  • Cauchy theory and regularity theory of models arising from the fluid mechanics
  • Fluid-structure interaction, fluid-solid system and singular limit
  • Fourier Analysis methods for PDEs, Lorentz spaces, Morrey spaces
  • Harmonic Analysis tools: singluar integrals
I am also working on some modelling and simulations of dynamics of PDEs (more details can be found Here).
